Parent Portal

Have a child who is over the age of 18 and he/she thinks they are now an adult?

You have done a fantastic job bringing up your child. You have sacrificed your entire life to ensure that your child has had a wonderful education.

Are you worried about their future?
Stressed that your child is not doing something useful with that education?
Frustrated hearing statements like “I am still young and am trying to figure things out” …


You are not alone! No child at that age wants to get told what to do by their parents.

If your child is in college or in their twenties, they will be tired of the constant pressure. Be it in a job interview or at a social get together they’re constantly asked about their future plans by everyone they meet in one form or another. They maybe a little confused with so many options available to them.

You know your child the best. You clearly know what they are good at and they struggle at. But your child is yet to make up their mind on what they want in life. Most of the important decisions in their life were taken by YOU. They have never had complete independence in taking their decisions.

Don’t you think, if your son or daughter gets the right direction and the right time your dream of seeing them doing better than you will be fulfilled.

We will show them a way to prioritize their goals better If they have many dreams, we will support them in chasing them one after another

Your child needs someone who can give them enough space while guiding them on finding their own unique way forward at their own pace. We are here to listen, be authentic in our feedback, and share our experience in a manner that propels them to discover and accomplish their Vision. We will share our know-how, wisdom and expertise with your child to process their dreams into their Vision.

The UBU team believes that all young adults like your child have dreams and they can achieve them. We are here to reassure you that for young adults like your child who are young, capable have the ability to create their dream life. Their Vision does not have to be something that needs a lot of effort or many years to form, it is accessible to them right now whenever they are ready.

We provide Personal Vision Coaching for young adults like your child. We work with them one-on-one to help them discover and forge their unique way forward. Our Mission is to help young adults live a more fulfilled life the way they want to.
