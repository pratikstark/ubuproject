What is UBU?

If you are in college or in your twenties, you might be tired of the constant pressure. Be it in a job interview or at a social get together you’ve been asked about your future plans by thousands of people in one form or another.

The very thought of long-term planning might be terrifying or frustrating. I am sure that you have contemplated this question in the past. After a bit of self-reflection, you might have buried it for later. Adult life is hard enough as it is. You might have told yourself “Let me get a job or my next promotion and then I can think about it.”

We are here to assure you that for young adults like you who are young, capable - you have the ability to create your dream life. Your Vision does not have to wait. Your Vision does not have to be something that needs a lot of effort or many years to form, it is accessible to you right now whenever you're ready.

We believe that you can do anything with your life
If you have many dreams, we will support you in chasing them one after another
We can help you in prioritizing your multiple talents
We can show you a way to manage your goals better


The UBU team believes that all young adults like you have dreams and you can achieve them. We will share our know-how, wisdom and expertise with you to process your dreams into your Vision. We are here to listen, be authentic in our feedback, and share our experience in a manner that propels you to discover your Vision.

We provide Personal Vision Coaching for young adults like you. We work with you one-on-one to discover and forge your unique way forward. Our Mission is to help you live a more fulfilled life the way you want to.
