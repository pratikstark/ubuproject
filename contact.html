FAQs

If you are reading this, you are thinking about creating a wonderful future. You might have questions like

  • What do I really want in life?
  • What kind of a life should I lead?
  • How do I create my dream life?
  • How can I make a difference?

A Vision will give you more clarity about


  • how you can create a life
  • the way you really want it
  • by taking charge of your life and
  • taking actions to live such a life

As Vision Coaches, we guide young adults like you to discover your Synchronous Vision in a systematic process. Vision Coaching will do the following for you.

  • Help you to process your dreams into your Vision
  • Facilitate you to take actions to move yourself towards your Vision
  • Guide you to discover what you really want in your life
  • Empower you to breakthrough obstacles which may stop you
  • Enable you to move forward in the right direction
  • Support you to achieve greater pace than you could on your own

Our ultimate aim is celebrating your success of achieving your Vision and living a happier and fulfilled life.

We at UBU believe that every young adult can lead a fulfilled life. This can be accomplished when you process your dreams into your Synchronous Vision. As Vision Coaches we will be focusing on your dreams related to all aspects of your life which include Career, Health, Family, Making a Difference (by helping others) etc.

We make it very easy for you to process your dreams into your Vision.

We are very happy to see young adults who are doing well in life. We hope that you have a Synchronous Vision which encompasses your career aspirations, health and fitness goals, great family and making a difference in the world.

If you think that you are doing really well in all of these aspects, then you might not need a Vision Coach. If you think that you want to develop a Synchronous Vision, then you can try Vision Coaching.

Financial goals are important in life and we have a session which deals with financial planning. We at UBU strongly believe that it is something every young adult has to learn. But life is more than just money and finances. Money can’t buy health or a good family.

We can help you with your goals irrespective of whether they are related to your finances, health, family, serving society or anything else which is important for you in life.

We are always excited to interact with young adults like you who have multiple goals in life. We can help you in prioritising them based on your own unique personality, perspective, circumstances etc. This will help you in achieving all your goals in a systematic manner.

Dreams don’t have an expiry date. If you have dreams and want to process them into a Vision, a Vision coach can help you. Age is just a number.

Anyone can benefit from UBU. It depends on how keen they are to take charge of their life.

Ofcourse. All Visions start off as a vague dream. If you are keen to process your dreams into your Vision, we can make it very easy for you.

Ambani started off as a petrol attendant. Rajnikanth was a bus conductor, Patricia Narayan who used to sell homemade pickles, jams and squashes owns a chain of restaurants in Chennai, Narendra Modi started off as a Chaiwala.

Everyone is blessed with different skills and resources. If you have big dreams and want to achieve them, we can support you to convert your dreams into your Vision.

Career Coaching focuses only on your career. As Vision Coaches we will be focusing on all your dreams which include Career, Health, Family, Making a Difference (by helping others) etc.

Career is very important, but it is just one part of your life and you need to work on all your dreams to live more fulfilled lives.

You are constantly busy. It is very hard for you to stop and consider your future, and where you want to be. The very idea of long-term planning is very overwhelming.

We have created a simple and efficient process for you to process your dreams into your Vision.

As Vision coaches, we ensure that you focus exactly on what you want, help you identify all options available to you (including a few that you may not have thought of yourself) and then break it down for you to achieve it faster.

We are dedicated 100% to you and your future.

If you have a desire to create a wonderful future for yourself where you can lead a happier and more fulfilled life and realize that you will stay focused and get there quicker with the support of a coach or a mentor, you will benefit tremendously by having a Vision Coach.

From our experience people from all walks of life benefit from Vision coaching. We have coached people of all ages from preschoolers to senior citizens. The only trait all these people have in common is their desire to create a wonderful future by being more open to change.

Each Vision Coaching session takes approximately 45-60 minutes. We ideally suggest that the sessions are done without too much of a gap between each other since the learnings from each lead to the next session. We are flexible and the sessions can be daily, few times a week or even once in a week depending on your schedule and the speed at which you want to proceed on your journey.

The 5 broad steps in your UBU journey are

  1. UBU Preview
  2. UBU Insight
  3. UBU Breakthrough
  4. UBU Experience
  5. UBU Blueprint

Each step has a specific objective towards arriving at your Vision. For some people the whole UBU journey may take as little as 7 sessions and for some others it will be more. It varies from person to person based on the individual’s dreams.

The coaching is designed to take place online. This has proven to be the most convenient way for young adults like you to fit coaching into your busy lives. We suggest that you find a quiet room with no distractions where you can totally focus on the conversation.

Face to face coaching is also available for young adults in Bangalore if they prefer this option.

To get the best from the Vision Coaching, you will need to have a positive attitude towards the achievement of your goals. The more open, trusting and honest you are with the coaches, the better will be the results for you. You need to have the time and commitment to think about the learning from each session and complete the tasks that will help you get the most from your next session.

Ultimately your level of commitment will directly influence the speed and extent to which your Vision will be realised. We are there to provide you with boundless motivation, support and encouragement within a confidential and non-judgemental environment.
